***Please Note:***
------------------

Tufts Bands contain at least 50% Tufts students. Tufts Student Groups that receive TCU or other University funding do not count as Independent Tufts Bands and must pay the University Funded Groups rate.

If a secondary engineer is required to help with the session because of the size or complexity of the set-up, the charge will be increased by \$5/hr.

Mastering
---------

We provide mastering services through Sonic Enhancement Specialists at the following rates.

Single (max 3 songs): \$150
 EP (max 6 songs): \$200
 Full Length (up to 12 songs): \$300

Contact [ontheside@wmfo.org](mailto:ontheside@wmfo.org "mailto:ontheside@wmfo.org") for more details.

CD Production
